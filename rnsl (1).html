PLAYER STATS
The Convicts
⚽ NEYMAR COMPARISON

The Convicts are a force to be reckoned with on the pitch. Aggressive from the first whistle, they press hard and take risks that most teams wouldn't dare. What sets them apart is the technical quality behind that aggression — this is a playmaker in the truest sense, capable of threading passes through tight spaces and creating chances out of nothing. Like Neymar, they combine flair with intensity, making them unpredictable and dangerous. When they're on, they're one of the most exciting teams in the league to watch.

Stars
⚽ ANDREA PIRLO COMPARISON

Stars don't rush. They don't panic. They don't make mistakes. Every touch is deliberate, every decision calculated with surgical precision. Like Andrea Pirlo, they read the game two steps ahead of everyone else — controlling the tempo, picking the right moment, and executing flawlessly when it matters. Their unbeaten record is no accident. It's the product of a team that simply refuses to beat themselves. Cool, composed, and quietly dominant.

The Vampires
⚽ RONALDINHO COMPARISON

The Vampires know exactly what they're doing at all times. Their game sense is elite — they see angles others don't, make decisions at lightning speed, and have a natural feel for the game that can't be taught. Like Ronaldinho, they play with a smile on their face and a trick up their sleeve, combining high footballing IQ with moments of pure magic that leave opponents flat-footed. They don't just play the game — they understand it on a deeper level, and it shows every time they step on the pitch.

SMD FC
⚽ KYLIAN MBAPPE COMPARISON

SMD FC have clicked — and the results are showing it. A perfect 3-0 record proves that the potential was always there, and now it's being fully realized. Like Mbappé, they have an explosiveness that catches opponents off guard, and once they get going there's very little anyone can do to stop them. The breakthrough everyone saw coming has arrived, and SMD FC are now one of the most dangerous teams in the league.

The Islanders
⚽ MARIO BALOTELLI COMPARISON

The Islanders come in hot every single game — aggressive, physical, and impossible to ignore. They throw themselves into every challenge and play with a reckless energy that can be thrilling to watch. A lot of what they do relies on fortune going their way, and sometimes it does — those moments of genuine quality, a perfectly struck ball out of nowhere, remind you why you never count them out. Like Mario Balotelli, they're infuriating and brilliant in equal measure. Unpredictable to the last.

Mijo FC
⚽ SERGIO RAMOS COMPARISON

Mijo FC play with an intensity that borders on reckless. Highly physical, they impose themselves on every game and make sure their opponents know they've been in a match. The red cards keep coming — a testament to a team that simply does not know how to dial it back. Like Sergio Ramos, they combine genuine quality with a win-at-all-costs mentality that gets them in trouble as often as it gets them results. Feared, controversial, and never boring.
